That smooth scroll is one of the things!

So what else does it do? I'm glad you ask. Sweet Links take a little effort out of some of the things you might wanna do with links, such as:

  • (✓) Smooth-scrolling to anchor links on the same page
  • (✓) Automatically adds target="_blank" to links if they are to external destinations (some people like this, some don't - it's optional)
  • (✓) Automatically adds the rel="external" attribute if the anchor links to an external destination.
  • (x) Automatically adds the Download attribute if the anchor links to a downloadable file, such as a PDF, Word Doc etc.
  • (✓) Automatically adds the title attribute to all of your links, if it doesn't exist.
